About Our School

A Legacy Of Excellence
Education At Its Finest

D.A.V Police Public School, Madhuban is a glittering bead in the rosary of the DAV Institutions run by DAV College Managing Committee, New Delhi.

The school is on the path of continuous development and progress. The school is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. About 65 dedicated committed, experienced teachers are moulding the destiny of more than 1200 students. The school has its own beautiful campus located in the heart of the city.
