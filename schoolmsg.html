About Our School

20 Years Of Experience
In Teaching Sector

D.A.V Police Public School, Madhuban is a glittering bead in the rosary of the DAV Institutions run by DAV College Managing Committee, New Delhi.

The school is on the path of continuous development and progress. The school is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. About 65 dedicated committed, experienced teachers are moulding the destiny of more than 1200 students. The school has its own beautiful campus located in the heart of the city. Properly constructed & well furnished class rooms and Faculty room highly speak of the facilities of modern amenities available in this seat of learning. School consists of Physics Laboratory, Biology Laboratory, Chemistry Laboratory, Computer Laboratory, Mathematics Laboratory, Social Science Laboratory, Language Lab Library, Dance Room Art and Craft Room, Room For Indoor Games, Medical Room, Yoga Room, Music Room, Activity Room, well equipped Conference room with multi-media projectors. Text based learning is supplemented with audio-video resources like wide teleconferences and Internet facility for the students and faculty.
